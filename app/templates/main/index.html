Home Affordability with Airbnb

Home affordability continues to be a major challenge in many metropolitan areas. With housing prices outpacing wages and escalating student debt levels, it has become increasingly difficult for prospective home buyers to fulfill the American dream.


Fortunately, homeowners now have the option to Airbnb spare rooms in their new homes to help pay for the mortgage.


Using machine learning models, this projects aims to estimate the monthly income from renting our a room on Airbnb.
